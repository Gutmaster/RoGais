====== Pirate Treasure Race ======
Created Thursday 08 March 2018

The player must compete with Lady Pirate to chase down a relic in a dungeon located in [[Regions:Coastal Rockery|Coastal Rockery.]] This event culminates in a battle vs. Lady Pirate and her goons. Completing the event unlocks her and the relic.
