====== Front ======
Created Tuesday 05 December 2017

The front row is the only row in which most [[Combat:Melee Attacks|Melee Attacks]] may be performed. It is generally the most vulnerable row because as long as you have a [[Units|unit]] residing in it, it is the only row subject to enemy Melee Attacks.
