====== Froggodile ======
Created Friday 30 March 20

A poisonous amphibian that draws his prey nearer to him with his tongue. 

Actions: 
Tongue Snatch UR{Any} TR {B, M} AP 3 atkMod -1
Deals physical damage and pulls the target forward one row.

Poison Jaws UR{Front} TR {Melee} AP 3 Poison 1
Deals physical damage and poisons the target.
