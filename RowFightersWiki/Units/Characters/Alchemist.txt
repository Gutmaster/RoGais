====== Alchemist ======
Created Saturday 12 May 2018

Passive Abilities:
Transmute - Convert items to gold on party menu.

Active Abilities:
Petrify - Turns target into stone.
Gild - Turns target into gold, safe effect as petrify, but awards gold if target is defeated while under the status effect.
Unstable form - Turns target into a random enemy.
Pacify - Turns target into a harmless creature. (Insta-kill type effect)
Golem - Turns an ally into a powerful golem with set Attributes and Abilities for x turns.
