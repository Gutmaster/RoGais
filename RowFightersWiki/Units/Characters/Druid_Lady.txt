====== Druid Lady ======
Created Wednesday 24 January 2018

Abilities:

Ensnaring Vines: User Row{Any}, Target Row{Any} - Prevents units from leaving the afflicted row.

Minor Beast: User Row{Any} - Summons an AI controlled beast in the unit's current row to fight for your side.

Nature's Blessing: User Row{Any}, Target Row{Any} - Target row applies healing and attribute buffs to all occupants.
